Our Return Policy

At PJ Export LTD, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return an item, please follow the guidelines below:

Return Eligibility

Items can be returned within 30 days of receipt, provided they are in their original condition, unused, and in the original packaging.

Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service at pjexport@yahoo.com to request a return.
  2. We will provide you with a return authorization number.
  3. Pack the item securely, include your return authorization number, and ship it to the address provided.

Return Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for the return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part (e.g., wrong item shipped).

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we will process it within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Exchanges

Unfortunately, we do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, please return it and place a new order.
