Our Story

Who We Are

PJ Export LTD is more than just an exporter of used clothes, shoes, and wiping rags—we are pioneers in sustainable textile solutions. Founded in 2006, our journey began with a single mission: to bridge the gap between surplus textiles and the global demand for affordable, high-quality, second-hand clothing. Today, we’ve evolved into a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, providing expertly sorted and graded textiles to meet diverse needs.

Our Growth and Milestones

From humble beginnings, PJ Export LTD has grown into a global player in the textile recycling industry. Our milestones include:

  • 2006: Founded with a focus on ethical and sustainable recycling.
  • 2009: Expanded our operations to reach 10 countries across Africa and Asia.
  • 2015: Opened our state-of-the-art sorting facility, increasing processing capacity.
  • 2020: Surpassed 10 million pounds in recycled textiles, contributing to significant waste reduction.
  • Present: Serving over 30 countries, providing tailored solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission and Ethical Practices

At the core of our operations is a commitment to sustainability. We believe in giving textiles a second life, reducing the environmental footprint of the fashion industry. Our comprehensive sorting process ensures that every garment, shoe, and rag is graded to maximize its potential for reuse or repurposing.

Our dedication to sustainability goes hand-in-hand with our ethical business practices. We partner with suppliers and clients worldwide to ensure fair wages and safe working conditions throughout the supply chain. By promoting responsible and transparent business practices, we aim to create a lasting, positive impact both environmentally and socially.

Global Reach and Future Goals

Today, PJ Export LTD’s reach extends to every corner of the globe. We are proud to collaborate with businesses of all sizes, from small retailers to large corporations, ensuring a steady supply of high-quality, second-hand textiles. As we continue to grow, our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices remains stronger than ever.

Looking ahead, our goal is to further reduce textile waste by refining our processes and expanding into new markets. By doing so, we aim to lead the way in creating a greener and more sustainable future for the textile industry.

Our Mission & Values

Quality

We are committed to providing the highest quality products. Each item is carefully sorted and graded by our expert team to ensure it meets the specific needs of our diverse global clientele. From second-hand clothing to industrial wiping rags, quality is our top priority.

Sustainability

Our mission is to promote recycling and reduce textile waste. We believe in giving textiles a second life and actively work to reduce the environmental impact of the fashion industry. Through our efforts, we contribute to a more sustainable world by lowering landfill waste and conserving resources.

Global Reach

With operations in multiple continents, we have developed a global network that ensures we can meet the needs of businesses anywhere in the world. Our logistical capabilities allow us to deliver products efficiently and reliably, ensuring our clients receive their orders on time, every time.
