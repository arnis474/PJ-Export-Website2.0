About Us

PJ Export LTD is a global leader in the export of used clothes, shoes, and wiping rags. Founded in 2006, we have grown to become a trusted partner for businesses around the world. Our commitment to sustainability and recycling has allowed us to make a significant impact in the textile industry, reducing waste and promoting reuse. We pride ourselves on delivering top-quality products, carefully sorted and graded by our expert team. Our clients span across multiple continents, and our shipping capabilities ensure timely deliveries wherever you are located.

What We Offer

Wiping Rags:

Wiping rags are essential in various industries for cleaning, polishing, and absorbing spills. Our high-quality wiping rags are made from recycled textiles, making them a cost-effective and environmentally friendly solution for industrial and commercial use.

We offer a wide range of materials, including cotton, fleece, and mixed rags, to meet your specific needs. All our rags are expertly sorted, cut, and graded to ensure maximum absorbency, durability, and efficiency.

Used Clothing:

We are proud to offer a wide range of used clothes, sorted into different categories for global resale markets. Our clothes are carefully selected and graded, ensuring that only the best quality garments are offered to our customers.

From children’s wear to adult fashion, our selection covers a wide spectrum of styles, sizes, and conditions. We work closely with our suppliers to ensure that all clothes meet high standards of cleanliness and wearability.
